Event Decision-Making Simulation

Planning to execution under uncertainty

Suggested Time: 40:00

Instructions

You are the event manager of LeadForward 2026, a university youth leadership fair with 300 expected participants. You will go through the planning, pre-event, and execution stages. Sudden incidents will appear. Your task is to make decisions and determine whether the event should proceed, be adjusted, postponed, or canceled.

This is an individual asynchronous simulation. Complete all stages and submit:

  1. A screenshot of your final results page
  2. Your downloaded decision log file

At the end, include your AI disclosure if required by your instructor.

Initial Scenario

  • Expected participants: 300

  • Date: Tomorrow, 9:00 AM to 4:00 PM
